About

I'm a former techical support technician with a passion for problem solving and connecting people with technology.

I was drawn to software engineering for its perfect blend of technical structure creativity; if you can imagine it, it can be realized (with a lot of reasearch)! I love being able to create something useful starting from little more than a blank screen, a blinking cursor, and a little inspiration.

I'm also excited about never having to reset anyone's password again :)

I got my start with an intensive coding bootcamp, App Academy. The first hurdle was getting in; they only accept 3% of all applicants. From there, I poured 24 weeks and more than 1000 hours into learning how to code in JavaScript & Python, work with some of their frameworks & libraries (Flask FTW!), and then make it all look pretty with a dash (read: heaping pile) of CSS.

It was a lot of hard work and sacrifice but the results have been incredible! I stretched the limits of my physical, mental and emotional stamina and proved to myself that I'm capable of anything. In the span of 12 weeks I was able to create four fullstack applications, two of which I made with the help of an amazing team of developers.

I'm overjoyed to be stepping into the next chapter of my life doing something I love; I get to solve puzzles all day!

It's only up from here and the sky is the limit.
